In the digital age, a teacher's role extends beyond imparting knowledge; it transforms into guiding students to navigate a world overflowing with information. A good teacher inspires curiosity, critical thinking, and ethical responsibility, helping students embrace technology as a tool for growth and innovation. By fostering collaboration over competition, they create an environment where learners feel empowered to explore, question, and create. The digital world demands resilience—the ability to learn, unlearn, and relearn—and teachers play a crucial role in nurturing this adaptability. Above all, they instill values that ensure technology is used responsibly, shaping not just skilled professionals but conscientious global citizens..
